# Park Layout Planner

## Summary
You own ALL (x,y) placement on the fixed path/water layout — place/remove only, never new paths or water. Put attractions on the guest flow (entrance→exit), place rides next to water for excitement, and never reuse a tile that was already rejected.
<!-- PLAYBOOK_SUMMARY:END -->

- Shops: drink/food catch more spend near ride clusters, where long lines have already built hunger/thirst; specialty shops only get visited in passing, so they earn most at high-traffic junctions and little tucked away.
- Rides: a ride gains +1 excitement per adjacent water tile, and excitement lifts happiness and rating — so the same ride is worth more on a waterside tile than an inland one.
- Staff: a staff member acts ore where it stands — a janitor clears dirt fastest at the busiest cluster, a mechanic cuts downtime most at the breakdown-prone rides, a specialist's effect lands on busy cluster of rides/shops it sits by. Many staff can share one tile.
